Mission:

The Smoke Free Lowcountry Coalition is committed to the reduction of tobacco use and exposure across the Lowcountry through public education and advocacy of healthy policy change.

Vision:

A healthy and tobacco free Lowcountry

Goals:

-To eliminate exposure to environmental tobacco smoke.
-To promote tobacco cessation among youth and adults.
-To prevent youth from initiating tobacco use.
-To identify and eliminate health disparities among our population.

Support a Smoke Free Lowcountry

-Smoke Free policies protect employees and customers from dangerous second-hand smoke
-Smoke Free policies facilitate a more productive workforce and environment
-Smoke Free ordinances in cities across America have proven that smoke-free environments are good for people and good for business
