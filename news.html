Please join us for the Smoke-Free Lowcountry Coalition's Annual Members' Meeting

Business Meeting:

Smoking in Multiunit Housing: Attitudes and Experiences with Secondhand Smoke Exposures among Key Stakeholders

Guest Speaker:

Andrea Gentzke
Roswelll Park Cancer Institute
Dept. of Health Behavior and Air Pollution Exposure Research

When?

Friday, May 1, 2015  9am - 11:00am
Free hors d'oeuvres will be served.

Where?

EQC Building
Hennesey Conference Room
1362 McMillian Avenue, Suite 300
North Charleston, SC 29405

Directions:

Take the Dorchester Road exit off of I-26, Exit 215 towards Hwy 52(Rivers Avenue). Turn left onto Rivers Avenue. Turn right onto McMillian between the old Navy Hospital and McDonald's. Follow McMillian, cross over Spruill and the railroad tracks. The building is on the corners of McMillian, Noisette, and Hobson. It is the new looking 4 story red brick building on the left hand side of McMillian. Parking is available on the Noisette side of the building in the large parking lot across the street.
