Smoke Free Ordinances have been passed in the City of Charleston, Mount Pleasant, Isle of Palms, Sullivan's Island, Ravenel, Hollywood, Summerville and Goose Creek! Enjoy smoke free dining in any restaurant within those city or town limits.

Smoke Free Dining is Just Good Taste

If you're like most Lowcountry residents, you'll agree smoke free dining just tastes better. Not only are smoke free eating establishments healthier for you and your family, but they're easier on your clothes, hair and skin and allow your taste buds to savor all the delicious flavors you've come to expect when you're enjoying a meal out on the town.

According to the EPA, a nonsmoking section is not enough. While exhaust systems installed in many restaurants' nonsmoking sections remove the smoky haze and odor, the systems do nothing to remove the 4,000+ toxic chemicals that have been identified in environmental tobacco smoke.

Restaurants are beginning to listen to their nonsmoking guests who are tired of avoiding particular establishments becuase of an asthmatic child, new baby or eldery parents with breathing problems. Even adults in perfectly good health can suffer the consequences of spending two hours breathing in secondhand smoke. Low levels of exposure can result in temporary infertility (both in men and women), blood clots, tooth decay, breathing problems and a host of other conditions.

Looking for a Smoke Free Restaurant in Your Neighborhood?

Maybe you have young children or problems with asthma. Maybe you are pregnant and worried about the harmful side effects of secondhand smoke on your unborn child, or maybe you just prefer to enjoy your food in a smoke free environment. Whatever your reason, you've joined the ranks of thousands of other Lowcountry residents who prefer clean air when they eat.

Next time you plan to have dinner out or are looking for a venue to hold that special occasion, check here to see if the restaurant is smoke free.

We are keeping a list of restaurants in the Lowcountry that are listening to their customers and choosing to go smoke free. Don't see your favorite restaurant on the list? Call them and ask what it would take for them to go smoke free and earn your business.
